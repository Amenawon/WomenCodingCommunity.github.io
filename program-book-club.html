Book Club Programme

What sets our book club apart are the lively conversations, valuable insights and an opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals. The books we read are selected by the community, offering a diverse selection of tech books and essential soft skills topics.

The book club meets monthly to share views, exchange ideas, and inspire personal and professional growth.

Join our slack channel #prog_book_club to be part of this vibrant community.
