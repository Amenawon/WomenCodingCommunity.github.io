About Us

Our story

Founded in 2024 in London, Women Coding Community emerged from a shared desire to create a supportive and empowering space for all women in the tech space. Our journey is one of resilience, collaboration, and empowerment, driven by the belief that together, we can shatter glass ceilings and pave the way for future generations of women in tech.

Our mission

At Women Coding Community we aim to amplify the voices, talents, and opportunities for women in the tech industry. We are committed to breaking barriers, advocating for gender equality, and creating a culture where every woman feels valued, supported, and empowered to thrive in tech.
